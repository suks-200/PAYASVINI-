About Jamehpur, Jharkhand

Jamehpur is a vibrant village in Jharkhand, known for its rich agricultural heritage and sustainable farming practices.
झारखंड का जैमपुर, कृषि और गोबर उत्पादों के लिए प्रसिद्ध है।
The region is famous for its high-quality cow dung, used in organic farming, biogas production, and eco-friendly products.
Prithu Payasvini Utpaad LLP is committed to sustainable innovation and premium quality.

Packaging & Usage

Our cow dung is carefully packed in moisture-proof, eco-friendly bags and boxes.
हम गोबर को सुरक्षित, स्वच्छ और इको-फ्रेंडली पैकिंग में भेजते हैं।
We use hygienic methods to ensure purity and quality. Each pack is sealed and labeled for export, with custom sizes available for bulk orders.
All packaging is certified and meets international standards.

  • Packaging: Vacuum-sealed bags, eco cartons, bulk sacks (वैक्यूम पैक, इको कार्टन, बल्क बोरी)
  • Usage: Organic farming, biogas plants, construction, religious rituals (जैविक खेती, बायोगैस, निर्माण, धार्मिक कार्य)
  • Export: International shipping with all certifications (अंतरराष्ट्रीय प्रमाणपत्र सहित)
